UPLIFTING LIVES NOW CHARITY FOUNDATION




Who We Are

UPLIFTING LIVES NOW CHARITY FOUNDATION is a Ghanaian based non-profit organization.

We focus on providing care and support for children. We empower local communities. Projects we undertake benefit the children, needy, sick and disabled and the community as a whole. Uplifting Liveds Now Charity Foundation was founded in December 2020. It's a non-profit organisation, dedicated for providing clean water to underserved communities. Since we're inspired by our mission, we work tirelessly to create meaningful, measureable change. Our aim is to ensure that every child has access to quality education, regardless of their background. We envision a world where opportunities are not limited by circumstances, and every individual can thrive. Through donations and partnerships, we are able to provide scholarships, build classrooms and equip students with essential learning tools. To date we've reached over 10,000 children in both the Oti region and Savannah region of Ghana. Did you know that 1 in 5 children worldwide lacks access to basic education? Your support helps break the cycle of poverty, empowering families and communities for generations. Since our founding in 2020, we've been able to provide clean drinking water to 20,000 families, built 15 schools in rural areas across Ghana and partnered with over 15 organisations worlwide to expand our reach. Every donation, no matter how small, brings us closer to our goal. Join us today to make a lasting difference. Together, we can change lives.


Our Mission

Our mission at Uplifting Lives Now Charity Foundation is to create lasting change by providing basic neccessities to less priveleged individuals and homes. We are dedicated to empowering individuals and communities through initiatives such as; education, healthcare, food relief and many more... ensuring that no one is left behind. With the support of our donors and partners, we strive to build a brighter, more equitable future for all, one step at a time.
